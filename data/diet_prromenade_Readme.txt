Phenotype = animal-based or plant-based diet at a particulat day in the experiment
Feature = KEGG Enzyme Code (EC), associated with protein domain sequences from IBM Functional Genomics Platform
EC mapping to enzyme names and pathways: https://www.genome.jp/brite/ko01000

PRROMenade processing of human gut microbiome data related to diet is described in this IBM Research paper:
https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2589004220301723
Utro et al. "Hierarchically Labeled Database Indexing Allows Scalable Characterization of Microbiomes" 

Original diet microbiome data is from this external paper:
https://www.nature.com/articles/nature12820
David et al. "Diet rapidly and reproducibly alters the human gut microbiome"
